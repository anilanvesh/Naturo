Privacy Policy for Naturo

Last Updated: July 2026

Naturo ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through the App.

1. Information Collection and Use

Naturo does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics SDKs to monitor your behavior or usage patterns.
  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account or provide any login credentials to use the App.

2. Permissions

The App requests specific permissions to provide its core functionality:

  • Foreground Service & Media Playback: Used to keep sounds playing while the App is in the background or when your screen is locked.
  • Notifications: Used to show playback controls (Play/Pause/Stop) in your device's notification tray for easy access.

3. Data Storage

Any settings you adjust (such as volume levels or timer preferences) are stored locally on your device only. This data is not uploaded to any servers and is deleted if you uninstall the App.

4. Third-Party Services

Naturo does not include any third-party advertising, analytics, or social media plugins. No data is shared with third parties because no data is collected.

5. Offline Functionality

The App is designed to work entirely offline. All sound resources are contained within the App package, and no internet connection is required for playback.

6. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information, Naturo is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect or solicit any information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the contact information provided in the Google Play Store listing.
